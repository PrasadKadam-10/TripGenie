AULI – Snow-Covered Himalayan Ski Destination

Auli, located in Uttarakhand, is a beautiful mountain destination known for its snow-covered slopes, alpine forests, and breathtaking Himalayan views. Situated at an altitude of about 2,800 meters, Auli is one of India’s top skiing destinations and offers mesmerizing views of peaks like Nanda Devi, Mana Parvat, and Kamet. The peaceful surroundings and cool climate make it a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

The Auli Ropeway, one of the longest cable cars in India, provides spectacular aerial views of snow-clad mountains and valleys. Visitors can enjoy skiing in winter, trekking, camping, and nature photography throughout the year. The nearby Gurso Bugyal and artificial lake in Auli add to the scenic charm of the destination.

The best time to visit Auli is from December to March for snow activities and April to June for pleasant mountain weather. By Road: Well connected from Joshimath and Rishikesh. By Rail: Rishikesh Railway Station (approx. 250 km). By Air: Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun (approx. 270 km). Travelers should carry warm clothing, trekking gear, and book accommodations in advance during peak tourist season.
