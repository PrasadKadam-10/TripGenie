Darjeeling is a scenic hill station located in the eastern Himalayas, famous for its tea plantations, cool climate, and breathtaking views of Kanchenjunga, the third-highest mountain in the world. Often called the “Queen of the Hills,” it is known for attractions like the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway (Toy Train – UNESCO World Heritage), Tiger Hill sunrise viewpoint, Batasia Loop, and Peace Pagoda. The region is globally renowned for its premium Darjeeling tea and lush green landscapes. It is an ideal destination for nature lovers, photographers, and those seeking a peaceful mountain retreat. The best time to visit is from March to June and October to December when the weather is clear and pleasant.
